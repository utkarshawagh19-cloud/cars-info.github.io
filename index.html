Types of Cars

Sports Cars

Sports cars are built for speed, performance, and luxury. They have powerful engines, aerodynamic design, and premium interiors.

Electric Cars

Electric cars use batteries instead of fuel. They reduce pollution and offer quiet, smooth driving.

SUVs

SUVs are strong, spacious vehicles designed for families, off-road adventures, and long journeys.

Sedans

Sedans are comfortable and fuel-efficient cars ideal for daily commuting and city driving.
